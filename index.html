Meet Mycroft

Every night, a small grey cat named Mycroft pads quietly through a famous city, pausing at its grand landmarks to say goodnight. Written in the gentle rhythms of a bedtime rhyme, each book in the Goodnight Cities series helps little ones wind down with a cosy journey to somewhere new.

Whether your child dreams of London's red buses or New York's glittering skyline, Mycroft is there to tuck them in — one city at a time.
